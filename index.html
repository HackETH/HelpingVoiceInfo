Helping Voice is about sharing moments and having fun. Our goal in creating these rules is to accommodate the broadest range of self expression while balancing the need for our users to be able to use our service safely and enjoyably.

Blocking & Reporting

If you think that your chatpartner doesn't follow our rules you have the ability to either Block or Report him. When you block your chatpartner you will no longer get any photos from him. When you think that your Helping Voice partner clearly violates our Rules, please report him via the settings section in the app. We will then look into the issue.

Alltough you can use the report feature to get a new match before your 24 hours have ended, we strongly discourage you from doing so if there's no reason to report your chatparter.
